Cordoba, Argentina

Córdoba is a city in central Argentina located at the foothills of the Sierras Chicas along the Suquía River, about 700 km (435 miles) northwest of Buenos Aires. It is the capital of Córdoba Province and the second-largest city in Argentina after Buenos Aires, with approximately 1.6 million people living in the metropolitan area. The city is well known for its universities, rich history, vibrant culture, and beautiful natural surroundings.
